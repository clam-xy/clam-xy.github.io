Network of philosophers
If you would like to gain some insights of philosophers, the wise and mysterious group of people, then you are at the right place!

Abstract

The purpose of this final projct is to answer some questions: Who is the most important philosopher? What are the philosophers thinking? Does philosophy changes with time going on? To answer them, we create two datasets and three analysis:

  • datasets with philosophers' name and their attributes
  • three analysis including: analysis of attributes of philosophers, analysis of connection among some attributes and analysis of philosophers' theory&philosophical tradition.

Datasets can be download here and explainer notebook is shown here. Below is introduction to our datasets and brief of each analysis, you can also check our detail studies directly here.

Introduction to three analysis:

There are three analysis focus on: philosohpers' attributes, connection of attributes and philosohpers' thought separately. All of them in studies, you can read them completely.

  • In the analysis of attributes, three attributes: region, era and core area are analysed separately to find the most import philosopher or class in each attribute. The method or tool including: directed network, word-cloud(also known as tag cloud).

  • In the analysis of connection of attributes, it focus on the connections among theories and research fields and the trend of these three attributes in three eras. The method or tool including: weight network and heatmap. You can also read the details here.

  • In the analysis of philosophers' theory&philosophical tradition, two attributes: theory&tradition are analysed separately and some representative books of some famous philosophers are analysed. You can read detail page here
